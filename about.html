Tamsin coming through, from the sunny island of Malta. With a penchant for the sea, and yet an even stronger desire not to get my feet wet, I am, needless to say, a mess of contradictions rolled into a tiny, unruly-haired -some say curly, I'm just being honest- person.

The day following my graduation, I hopped on a plane to Paris in hopes of achieving my life-long dream: that of living in the city of Light and Love, in spite of my eternal fear of Ratatouille and Co. I have been living in Paris ever since, and for the sake of my sanity, dear reader, I will not divulge how long it has been!

This space is all about sharing bits of myself with you: be it of my love for anything blue (though mostly skies and seas), of my inner ramblings, or of my latest discovery of the world of coding.
Hope you have an entire packet of paracetamol to spare! Cheers!
